ADVANCING AGRICULTURE

XAG is dedicated to bringing drones, robots, autopilot, artificial intelligence, and Internet-of-things into the world of agricultural production. It creates a smart agriculture ecosystem that leads us into the era of Agriculture 4.0 characterised as automation, precision, and efficiency.

Mission

Advancing Agriculture

Vision

We aim to build the infrastructure of agriculture for the next 100 years, that will provide the world with sufficient, diversified, and safe food.

Timeline

2020

Endowed with the new mission of advancing agriculture that strives to build the infrastructure of agriculture for the next 100 years, that will provide the world with sufficient, diversified, and safe food.

2019

Plant Protection UAS upgraded into Agricultural UAS with the launch of JetSeed Granule Spreading System; Released Unmanned Ground Vehicle, Autopilot Console and Smart Agriculture System; Awarded the Best Innovation in Precision Farming Technology at Crop Science Awards.

2018

Rolled out the new model P30 Plant Protection UAS with international glories, while obtaining a market share of over 50% in China’s crop protection UAS industry. XAG Cloud became the only agricultural drone management system authorised by CAAC.

2017

The world’s first fully autonomous plant protection UAS P20 debuted, enabling centimetre-level accuracy operation based on RTK network and digital field maps; founded subsidiary XGEOMATIC and XIOT with the development of mapping drones and agriculture IoT system.

2016

Launched the P Series Plant Protection UAS and Intelligent Rotary Atomisation Spraying System (iRASS); Founded XAG Academy for agricultural talent training.

2015

Launched the first generation of plant protection UAS and founded XPLANET which became one of largest service team of UAS crop protection.

2014

XAIRCRAFT renamed as XAG, focusing on the development and manufacture of agricultural drone.

2013-2012

Explored the industrial applications of drones in scientific expedition, inspection, search, and rescue and logistic; then started to dive deep into the agriculture sector.

2011

Launched the revolutionary SUPERX flight control system.

2010-2008

Dedicated to the development of multirotor drone with the launch of the industry-leading X650 series unmanned aerial system.

2007

On April 1, a group of flight enthusiasts lead by Peng Bin established XAG – formerly XAIRCRAFT.
